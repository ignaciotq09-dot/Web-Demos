Our Story

A Memphis Tradition for Over 35 Years

For over three decades, A-Tan's Hibachi Steakhouse has been a cornerstone of Midtown Memphis dining. What started as a small family restaurant has grown into one of the city's most beloved destinations for authentic Chinese and Japanese cuisine.

Located just minutes from the University of Memphis, the Pink Palace Museum, and the Memphis Zoo, A-Tan's is where Memphians come to celebrate, gather, and enjoy an unforgettable dining experience.
