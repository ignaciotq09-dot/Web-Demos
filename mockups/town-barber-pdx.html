Our Story

Three Generations of Craft

Town Barber PDX carries on a proud family tradition. Jason Townsend brings years of expert barbering experience with a flair of his own style, continuing what his father and grandfather started decades ago.

Conveniently located next to the new Ritz Carlton in downtown Portland, we blend old-school craftsmanship with modern style. Every cut gets the full attention it deserves — quality you can see and feel.
