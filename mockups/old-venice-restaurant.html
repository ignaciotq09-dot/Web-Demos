Our Story

Where Tradition Meets Romance

For over four decades, Old Venice has been San Diego's hidden gem in the heart of Point Loma. What started as a small neighborhood restaurant has grown into a beloved institution known for its old-world charm and exceptional Italian cuisine.

"The perfect place to enjoy time with the ones you love."

Whether you're celebrating a milestone, enjoying date night, or gathering with friends, our warm atmosphere and attentive service make every visit a cherished memory.
