Our Legacy

50+ Years of Excellence

Founded in 1972, America's Best Electric has been a cornerstone of Houston's electrical industry for over half a century. What started as a one-man operation has grown into one of the most trusted electrical companies in Texas.

Three generations of master electricians have carried forward our founding principles: quality workmanship, honest pricing, and unwavering commitment to safety.

1972

Founded in Houston, TX. Started with residential electrical services.

1985

Expanded into commercial electrical contracting. Grew to a team of 15.

2000

Second generation takes the helm. Modernized operations and expanded service area.

2020

Third generation joins. Added EV charging and smart home services.

Today

50,000+ jobs completed. Still family-owned, still Houston's best.
