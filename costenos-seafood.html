Our Story

Where Every Dish Tells a Story

At Costeños Seafood, we bring the vibrant flavors of coastal Latin America to beautiful Key Biscayne. Our chefs source the freshest seafood daily, preparing each dish with authentic recipes passed down through generations.

From our signature ceviches to our perfectly grilled whole fish, every plate is a celebration of the sea. Whether you're joining us for a casual lunch or a special dinner, you'll feel the warmth of our hospitality in every bite.
