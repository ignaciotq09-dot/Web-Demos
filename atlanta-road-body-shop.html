Our Story

Family Owned, Expert Crafted

Atlanta Road Body Shop has been serving the greater Atlanta metro area for over 20 years. As a family-owned and operated business, we treat every vehicle like it's our own — with care, precision, and pride.

Our I-CAR Gold Class certification means our technicians are trained to the highest industry standards, using state-of-the-art computerized frame measuring systems and environmentally safe waterborne paint.
